About GNDEC

Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College (GNDEC), established in 1956 under the aegis of Nankana Sahib Education Trust, is one of the oldest and most prestigious institutions in Northern India. Founded with the vision of uplifting rural students through technology, it continues to serve with excellence in technical education.

Affiliated with IKGPTU and approved by AICTE, GNDEC holds autonomous status and is accredited with ‘A’ grade by NAAC. It offers a wide range of UG, PG, and Ph.D. programs with a strong emphasis on research, innovation, and values-based education.
